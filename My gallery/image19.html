Industry 4.0

Industry 4.0 represents the fourth industrial revolution, where smart technologies, automation, and data-driven systems are transforming manufacturing and production.

Key innovations include the Internet of Things (IoT), artificial intelligence, cyber-physical systems, and big data analytics. These technologies help improve efficiency, optimize supply chains, and enable predictive maintenance.

With the integration of robotics, cloud computing, and digital twins, Industry 4.0 is paving the way for smart factories and intelligent industrial operations, leading to greater productivity and innovation.
